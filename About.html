About Us

The School Management System was made to help the departments of Pasig Catholic College plan, approve, and keep track of things in a more organized and easier way. It serves as a one-stop platform where departments can submit their event proposals, include activity details and budgets, and check the status of their approval all in one place.

This system helps improve coordination among college departments, ensuring that every event is properly planned, scheduled, and budgeted. By going digital, it minimizes manual mistakes and promotes a more transparent and efficient approval process.
