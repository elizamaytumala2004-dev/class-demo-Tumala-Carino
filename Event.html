Dancing

The dance event lets students show their talent, confidence, and creativity through movement and music. It’s a fun way to express emotions, entertain others, and build teamwork while enjoying every beat and step on stage.

Band

The band event gives students a chance to share their love for music and perform on stage. It’s all about teamwork, rhythm, and passion as they play instruments and sing together. This event brings energy and good vibes, creating a fun and memorable experience for everyone.

Games

The games event is all about fun, laughter, and friendly competition. It gives students a chance to relax, play, and bond with others while showing teamwork and quick thinking. It’s a great way to enjoy the event and create happy memories together.

Sports

This event is all about fun, talent, and teamwork, Students get the chance to show what they’ve got through dance performances, exciting sports competitions, and enjoyable games. It’s a time to celebrate school spirit, build friendships, and make lasting memories while learning the value of teamwork, confidence, and having fun together.

Pageant Competition

The pageant competition gives students a chance to shine with confidence, talent, and personality. Each course presents their own representatives who showcase not just beauty, but also intelligence and school pride. It’s an inspiring event that celebrates self-expression, creativity, and unity among students.

Intramurals

Intramurals is one of the most exciting events of the year where students compete in different sports and activities. It’s all about teamwork, school spirit, and friendly competition. This event gives everyone a chance to show their skills, build friendships, and enjoy a fun and active campus atmosphere.

Christmas Party

The Christmas Party is a joyful celebration filled with laughter, games, and sharing. It’s a time for students and teachers to relax, have fun, and spread love and kindness. The event brings everyone together to enjoy the spirit of giving and the happiness of the season.

Esports

The E-Game event lets students show their skills in online and video games. It’s a fun and exciting competition that builds teamwork, focus, and strategy. Players can enjoy friendly matches while connecting with others who share the same interest in gaming
